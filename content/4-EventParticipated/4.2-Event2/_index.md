# Event Summary: FCAJ Community Day on 23/05/2026

### Event Objectives
- Explore how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Large Language Models (LLMs) operate under the hood (the importance of context and the nature of determinism).
- Update cloud solutions and data platforms (Amazon CloudFront, Amazon QuickSight) to optimize performance and cost.
- Discover practical models in technology product development (via Hackathon competition) and enterprise-scale Multi-Agent System architecture application.

### List of Speakers
- **Tinh Truong** 
- **Anh Pham**
- **Thinh Nguyen** 
- **VIB Team** 
- **Duc Dao** 
- **Vy Lam** 

### Key Takeaways

#### 1. Platform Optimization with Amazon CloudFront
- **Versatile Platform:** Applying CloudFront across all types of workloads.
- **Core Advantages:** Cost optimization, robust security features, while delivering superior reliability and performance for the system.

#### 2. The 36-Hour Journey at LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho
- Sharing the 36-hour hackathon experience: Brainstorming, defining problem statements, and shaping the UTMorpho product.

#### 3. Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent Systems
- **Startup Credit Problem:** Structural asymmetry between bank data systems and startup company data.
- **Single-Agent vs. Multi-Agent:** Analysis of when to use a standalone Agent versus deploying a Multi-Agent model.
- **Virtual Credit Committee Model:** Architectural blueprint, security guardrails, regulatory compliance, ROI calculation, and implementation roadmap.


### Application to Work
- **Explore Multi-Agent Models:** Experiment with designing complex workflows by breaking them down into separate Agents (analysis, planning, synthesis) to optimize performance instead of assigning all tasks to a single Agent.
- **Embrace Hackathon Mindset:** Practice pinpointing core issues and launching rapid prototypes under time pressure to validate ideas.
