### General Assessment

The working environment is very friendly and open. The workspace is clean and comfortable, helping me focus better.

Mentors provide very detailed guidance, clearly explain concepts I didn't fully understand, and always encourage asking questions. The team admin supports administrative procedures, documentation, and facilitates a smooth working process.

**3. Alignment between Work and Major**  
The assigned work aligns well with what I learned at university, while allowing me to learn many additional AWS services.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools and teamwork skills. Mentors also shared practical experience that helped better guide my career orientation.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
Everyone in the team works very positively and proactively. Members are flexible in scheduling time to maintain weekly meetings. When difficulties arise, the team doesn't handle them individually; instead, we exchange ideas, analyze, and find the optimal solution together.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
Policies and benefits supporting interns are very good.
